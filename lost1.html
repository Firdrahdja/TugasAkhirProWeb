Vehicles



The 1964 chevy impala SS was destined to be in the game. It was to have usable hydraulics and a convertible roof, a first for the Need for Speed series. Unfortunately it was cut from the game due to programming difficulties. The Cadillac CTS, Porsche 911 Turbo (996), Mazda 3, Audi A4 3.2 Fsi Quattro, porsche 911 Carrera S (997), and the Volkswagen Golf GTi were also removed from the game for unknown reasons. The 911 Turbo's body remain as the ball car from Burnout Paradise. However, it can be fixed with the Improvement Mod of the game. Police helicopters were supposed to be present in pursuits like in Need for Speed: Most Wanted, but that was cut, again due to programming difficulties.

Gameplay



There are many cut features concerning gameplay, which were removed either due to time of programming difficulties. The graphics were meant to be better. Below is a list of cut features:

  • Traffic in Canyon Duel races
  • Canyon Duels were a lot more strict with any bumper contact meaning race lost.
  • Police helicopter with EMP disablers
  • EMP disablers
  • More varied weather
  • Customisable licence plates
  • Customisable crew car colours
  • AI crews were more active.
  • More than x20 multiplier in drift events
  • There were 5 pursuit breakers in the San Juan area
  • A fourth kind of crew member, a Persuader, which would get traffic to block an opponents path
  • Many motion capture scenes
  • 20 car races (race wars) were cut from previous generation consoles
  • A flag girl for Tuner car races
  • San Juan as a fully explorable district, with Police and AI
  • Ability to adjust the pitch of your car's suspension, making it slant downwards.
  • Drift with other AI racers

Cancelled Project


Need For Speed is a 25 year old game franchise that's still going to this day but years back there were some NFS that got cancelled, Unreleased & Overhauled. Their concept is so interesting for example the cancelled direct sequel of Most Wanted 2005. In this FanWeb you can find some remnants of those cancelled NFS.

Scrapped Concept



Those ideas were scrapped due to the one year development the publisher demanded. Because of the pressure from the publisher & deadline the developer had no choice but to scrapped some of the ideas that are going to be put in the game, remove some of the unfinished feature to prevent bugs & optimizing all the finished stuff thus leaving some bugs from the beta leftovers at the same time even though the developers already give their best.
